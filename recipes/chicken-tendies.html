Chicken Tenders Recipe

Desciption

The only thing more comforting would be fried butter but you're mildly overweight not obsese so we'll save that for when you've had enough of this mid life :(

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 lbs. of chicken tenders or 2 large boneless, skinless, chicken breasts. (sliced)
  • 1.5 cups of all purpose flour
  • 0.75 tsp. of salt
  • 1 egg beaten with 2 tbsp. of water
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Fill a cast iron or standard skillet with approximately 2 inches of oil
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt and pepper
  3. In another large bowl, beat the egg and water
  4. Dredge the chicken in the flour, coating well. Shake off excess flour and dip in the egg, then back in the flour
  5. Set the chicken to the side to rest approximatelt 5 minutes (helps the coating stick better)
  6. When oil temperature is steady between 365-375 you may begin laying in the coated tenders
  7. Cook the chicken for 8-10 minutes on each side. Golden brown bread is a good indicator of when to flip
  8. Transfer to a paper towel and optionally sprinkle with MSG while sizzling hot
