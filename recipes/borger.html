Burger Recipe

Description

Ever been hungry on a hot Summer's day wishing for something to fill you nad your boys?

Well look no further! Here at Abdul's Foodies Inc. we've concocted the most scientifically perfect recipe for that very burger!

Coupe it with a cold coke or a nice fresh lemonade and you've got yourself a craving killer for the next few hours (or at least unitl you want it again ;)

Ingredients:

  • 1 large egg
  • 0.5 tsp. of pink himalayan salt
  • 0.5 tsp. of ground black pepper
  • 1 lb. of ground beef
  • 0.5 cup of fine dry bread crumbs
  • Lettuce (To personal serving)
  • Tomatoes (Maximum of 2 slices/burger)
  • Onions (Maximum of 4 evenly sized rings)
  • McDonalds store bought secret sauce
  • Brioche buns

Steps:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate
  2. Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands or a fork until well blended. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties
  3. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C)
  4. Fresh off the grill transfer your patties to the bottom of our golden brown toasted brioche bun
  5. Lather a healthy serving of the store bought secret sauce. I prefer 1 tbsp. but to each his own
  6. Stack our lettuce leaf(s), onion ring(s), and tomato slices on top
  7. And finally top of with the second hald of our golden brown toasted brioche bun
